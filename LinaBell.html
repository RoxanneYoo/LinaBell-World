Introduction of LinaBell

LinaBell is an IP image released by Shanghai Disney on September 29, 2021. In the Disney story, Daffy met LinaBell after getting lost in the forest, and they became good friends. On the surface, this is a pink stuffed fox girl with big starry blue eyes and a big, fluffy fox tail. On her adventures, she uses her signature magnifying glass and always finds accomplishment and joy in solving problems and unraveling mysteries. Meanwhile, LinaBell loves nature and likes to spend quality time in the forest with Daffy and his friends.

LinaBell's Story

Designed by Ethan Reed

One day, Mickey and Duffy were walking in the forest. And a group of colorful butterflies flew towards me. Daffy got separated from Mickey while chasing a beautiful butterfly. Daffy is frustrated not knowing where he is or how to find Mickey. At this time, the pink fox girl in the forest happened to pass by and saw the frowning Duffy. She walked up to Duffy and asked, "What's wrong with you?". "I'm lost and can't get back to Mickey..." Daffy said.


The fox girl picked up her magnifying glass and looked at Daffy carefully. "Butterfly...orchid. That's it! I know where it is. I'll take you back." The fox girl who solved the mystery said confidently. "Really? You are so awesome!" Duffy turned her worries into joy. Daffy, who reunited with Mickey, said happily: "This fox girl is really smart!" The fox girl then introduced herself: "I am Lina Bell. My hobby is solving puzzles!" Finally, Duffy gave the orchid to Lina Bell as a small thank you.
